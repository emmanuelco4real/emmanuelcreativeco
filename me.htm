Frequently Asked Questions

What is your design process like? +
My process includes discovery (research & briefing), strategy, concept development, design execution, revisions, and final delivery.
How long does a typical project take? +
Depending on the project scope: Logos: 3–5 days Brand Identity: 1–2 weeks UI/UX Projects: 1–3 weeks Front-End Development: 1-9 weeks depending on complexity Full Branding + UI/UX: 2–4 weeks
What do you need from me to get started? +
A completed brief, business goals, brand name, any references/inspiration, and content (text/images, if available).
How many revisions are included? +
6 revision on final concept or agreement. More can me negotiated
Do you offer post-project support? +
Yes i offer post-project but only after 5weeks of project completion. Yes, I offer 14-21 days of free support after project completion, and extended support can be discussed if needed.
