My Design
Process

Crafting bold solutions that inspire and elevate brands through thoughtful process.

Research & Discovery

Strategy & Concept Development

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your design process like? +
My process includes discovery (research & briefing), strategy, concept development, design execution, revisions, and final delivery.
How long does a typical project take? +
Depending on the project scope:
Logos: 3–5 days
Brand Identity: 1–2 weeks
UI/UX Projects: 1–3 weeks
Front-End Development: 1-9 weeks depending on complexity
Full Branding + UI/UX: 2–4 weeks
What do you need from me to get started? +
Complete brief: business goals, brand name, any references/inspiration, and content (text/images, if available).
How many revisions are included? +
Up to 4 revisions are included on the final concept. Additional revisions can be discussed if needed.
Do you offer post-project support? +
I offer post-project support starting 5 weeks after completion. You’ll receive 14–21 days of free support, and extended help can be arranged.
