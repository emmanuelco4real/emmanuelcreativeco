Brand Identity Design For Gourguex Real Estate Brand

Problem:
Gourguex aimed to create an elegant trusted Real Estate brand but struggled with inconsistency in its branding. The company needed a strong brand identity that communicated Elegnce, Sophistication, Luxury, and reliability to Clients
Solution:
The designed logo features a Sophisticated yet professional mark, symbolizing Elegance. The Green and Golden reflects Elegance, Luxury, and Sophistication, making the brand approachable. A full 35-paged brand guideline ensures consistent brand communication across all platform, and advertising. This structured branding approach allows Gourguex to build a strong emotional connection with their Clients.
